This iconic rock anthem captivates me with its powerful guitar riffs and energetic rhythm. The driving beat and memorable chorus make it perfect for getting pumped up before any activity. It's a timeless classic that never fails to boost my mood.

K-POP

      K-Pop has taken the world by storm with its infectious beats, synchronized choreography, and visually stunning performances. This genre combines elements of electronic, hip-hop, and pop music with a uniquely Korean flair. Here are some of my favorite K-Pop tracks that showcase the genre's innovation and energy.

Dynamite

Artist: BTS

Single (2020)

How You Like That

Artist: BLACKPINK

What is Love?

Artist: TWICE

Album: Summer Nights (2018)

"What is Love?" by TWICE holds a special place in my heart because of its catchy melody and relatable lyrics about young love and curiosity. The song's bright and energetic vibe, combined with TWICE's signature synchronized choreography, never fails to lift my spirits. I particularly love how the music video pays homage to classic romantic movies while maintaining TWICE's cheerful charm. The chorus is incredibly addictive, and I find myself humming it throughout the day.
